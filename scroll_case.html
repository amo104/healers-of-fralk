Scroll case

Found on the outside right pocket of the backpack, this case too has naturally withstood the harsh climate of the Great Burial.

Object Information

Identification

  • Object Type

    container

  • Description

    A cylindrical leather container, stitched together with matching brown thread. The closure on the lid is shaped like the top of a leaf and straps onto the body of the container. The side of the container has a looping handle. All attachments to the cylinder are fastened with silver rivets.

Commentary

Although this scroll case was found carrying the map showcased in the exhibit, the case is notably much larger and older than the map. It is uncertain whether this case was made for an original map, or if the item is mundane.
